Trans-Continental Dance Collaborative

Or make a donation online through your PayPal account.

Visit Paypal, then select "send money" to dancefilms@msn.com

Benefits of donating include:

  • Your name or company logo will appear in performance programs and on the TCDC website.
  • Free membership to the Dance Films Association
  • Discounted admission to the Dance on Camera Film Festival
  • Complimentary subscription to the bi-monthly Dance on Camera Ezine
  • Support cross-cultural communication in the arts
  • Help bring dance film screenings to communities world-wide

Your donation to TCDC is tax deductible!

Trans-Continental Dance Collaborative is a fiscally sponsored project of the Dance Films Association, Inc., a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. For more info on DFA visit: http://www.dancefilmsassn.org
